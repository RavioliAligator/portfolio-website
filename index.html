G A L L E R Y

Visual arts student, storyboarder, and character designer

I’m Emma Harkin. Or just Jo.

Most of my work is very story and character driven. Recently, I’ve been doing a lot of traditional stand-alone pieces for school, but I’m enjoying the chance to complete high quality works while I decide what to pursue for a career. I think it’s important to write characters that extend beyond your artworks and have their own lives ‘offscreen.’
